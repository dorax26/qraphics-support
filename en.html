Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q. Which macOS versions are supported?
A. The app supports macOS 13.0 (Ventura) or later.

Q. I can't open the exported Excel file.
A. The Excel file exported by this app is in .xlsx format. It can be opened with Microsoft Excel, Apple Numbers, or other compatible software.

Q. What calculation method is used for the statistical tests?
A. Tukey's and Dunnett's tests are calculated using jStat, a standard statistical library.
